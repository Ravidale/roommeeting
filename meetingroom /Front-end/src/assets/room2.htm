Description

We want your conference to be a success and are confident that it will be if you decide to book our Manhattan meeting room rental. You will get to experience a top meeting room rental in this popular coworking space. The room can naturally also be used for seminars, conferences, brainstorming sessions and more. This Soho venue is located in a brand-new building with a very contemporary and stylish exterior. Large windows from floor to ceiling will not only let in plenty of natural light, but also allow you to enjoy precious views over Broadway and Howard. The recommended number of guests is 1 to 10 as they can then all be seated. In total, 20 people can fit in the meeting room for rent, 10 people would be standing, 10 seated.

The atmosphere in this space is very professional. Our aim was to create a welcoming, no-nonsense meeting room. We have maintained a clean and minimalistic approach for this meeting room rental to ensure a focused and attentive atmosphere for your presentation. For a more interactive setting feel free to write on the walls and even the table as this NYC meeting room includes Dry Erase equipment to support brainstorming sessions of any kind! It’s a fun and inspiring experience to be able to write down your ideas wherever and whenever they occur! That is why the place is perfectly suitable for an enterprises who wants to have a more interactive corporate meetings with a unique setting in order to increase productivity and creativity.

In terms of connectivity, our meeting room is equipped with state of the art A/V equipment including Apple TV and Chromecast. Water and coffee are already included in the rental price, with the ability for us to order you a hot lunch or the freedom to bring your own food.

We know that the location is very important for meeting rooms for rent, especially in NY. The location of our meeting room in Soho means that cool restaurants, hip fashion stores and a variety of museums and art galleries are only meters away. You should really plan to take advantage of this beautiful area and explore it after your meeting! You can easily reach our location with NYC public transportation system. Subway stops of line 4, 6, R, N and Q in direct vicinity.
